Holiday Evening 2025




Live through history, today. 

Experience an evening of wonder as Rock Ledge Ranch bursts with the sights, sounds, and aromas of this festive season! The evening features St. Nicholas, wagon rides, old-time musical entertainment, Blacksmith Shop demonstrations, Victorian celebrations in the 1880s Rock Ledge House, and Edwardian festivities in the 1907 Orchard House. Purchase special Ranch-made gifts at the General Store! Hot cider, cocoa, and other sweet treats will be available at several locations around the Ranch. Visit the Studio to make a special holiday craft.
